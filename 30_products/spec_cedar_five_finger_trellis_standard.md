## Standard Launch Definition

- target_dimensions: Approved launch dimensions - 70 in H x 32 in W x 2.25 in thick.
- standard_material_stack: One dog-eared cedar fence picket cut to 70 in, ripped into five 3/4 in uprights, with the leftover thin strip used for approximately 12 in, 24 in, and 34 in crosspieces.
- hardware_assumption: Exterior waterproof glue plus 18 gauge brad nails; exact nail count pending first build.
- drainage_assumption: N/A - trellis, not planter.
- liner_included: No
- finish_assumption: Unfinished cedar unless operator chooses a finish option.

For made-to-order products, small handmade variance does not automatically invalidate the stated standard size as long as the listing remains truthful. If actual builds show material drift, update the standard spec and listing wording before further sales.

## Fulfillment Baseline

- standard_lead_time_range: Lead time will be given when order is made.
- standard_delivery_policy: Pickup or local delivery by arrangement as a standalone item or add-on to any planter; standalone shipping not evaluated.
- standard_pickup_policy: Local pickup by arrangement.

## Included / Excluded

- included_in_base_version: One cedar five-finger fan trellis only; may be sold standalone or as an add-on to any planter.
- excluded_from_base_version: Planter, soil, plants, flowers, vines, delivery fee, installation, finish, and any attachment hardware unless the SKU F bundle explicitly includes attachment.

## Deviation Triggers Requiring Operator Confirmation

- deviation_1: Six-finger version instead of five-finger version.
- deviation_2: Physical attachment to a planter instead of loose or staged trellis placement.
- deviation_3: Any size, width, or crosspiece placement change from the draft source-based spec.
- deviation_4: Any finished, painted, stained, sealed, or weathered variant.
