## Shared Direction

- material_species: Pine unless a future approved variant states otherwise.
- available_colorways: NAT — Natural Wood with Red & Blue Stain; RUS — Rustic Torched Wood.
- NAT_definition: Natural wood light stripes and stars, with red and blue stained areas that retain visible pine grain.
- RUS_definition: No colored stain and no paint. Natural wood stars and natural/light stripes remain visible; the union receives the darkest torch treatment and alternating stripes receive darker torched contrast.
- shared_design: Wavy/sculpted American flag with 13 stripes and a 50-star layout.
- current_available_variants: USA1-L-NAT, USA1-M-NAT, USA1-S-NAT, USA1-L-RUS, USA1-M-RUS, USA1-S-RUS.
- unavailable_until_approved: Frame, draped, generic wood-burned option, Blacked Out, Black & Blue, thin-line, flag-cross, custom sizes, and any torched/burned style other than defined RUS Rustic Torched Wood.
