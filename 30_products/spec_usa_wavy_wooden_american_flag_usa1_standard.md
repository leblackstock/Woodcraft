## Standard Launch Definition

- target_dimensions: 24.7 in L x 13 in H x 1.5 in thick.
- product_format: Sculpted/wavy wooden American flag wall art.
- material_species: Pine.
- stripe_count: 13 horizontal stripes.
- color_direction: Red and blue flag with white/light elements represented by natural pine grain.
- natural_wood_elements: Light/white stripes and natural-pine star appearance target unless separately changed by the operator.
- union_direction: Blue union with 50-star American flag layout; exact star creation method pending.
- wave_direction: Wavy/sculpted flag face. Exact wave profile is not locked until the first USA1 build is documented.
- standard_material_stack: Pine stock; exact board choice, yield, strip method, and first-build cut/process record pending. Research references include small 24 in class 2x12 strip/grinder methods and larger 37 in 2x4 wave-template methods; USA1 must record the actual Drakkar material stack before repeat use.
- hardware_assumption: Pending. D-rings, keyhole hanger, sawtooth hanger, or other wall-hanging method must be chosen and recorded before listing.
- finish_assumption: Pending. Red/blue color system, clear coat, and indoor/outdoor wording must be confirmed before customer-facing copy.

For stock or made-to-order versions, small handmade variance does not automatically invalidate the stated standard size as long as the listing remains truthful. If actual builds drift materially from 24.7 in x 13 in x 1.5 in, update the product record, spec, and listing facts before further sales.

## Included / Excluded

- included_in_base_version: One wavy wooden American flag in the USA1 finish direction, with hanging hardware only if installed and verified.
- excluded_from_base_version: Installation, wall anchors/screws unless explicitly included, shipping, gift wrapping, custom engraving, alternate sizes, blacked-out variants, thin-line variants, and premium 37 in version.

## Deviation Triggers Requiring Operator Confirmation

- deviation_1: Any length/height/thickness change from 24.7 in L x 13 in H x 1.5 in thick.
- deviation_2: Blacked-out, thin blue line, thin red line, rustic/burnt, flag-cross, or other variant.
- deviation_3: Painted white elements instead of natural wood light/white elements.
- deviation_4: Change from 50-star American flag layout.
- deviation_5: Different hanging hardware or no ready-to-hang hardware after the listing implies hardware is included.
- deviation_6: Any indoor/outdoor/weather-resistant claim before finish and hardware support it.
