# 01 Vision

## Mission

Build and sell profitable handmade cedar products using a low-cost, repeatable system where AI helps with planning, scoring, drafting, and consistency.

## Success Definition

Success means:

- consistent weekly execution (not random bursts)
- profitable items prioritized over hobby projects
- faster listing/content production with less decision fatigue
- controlled ad spend with clear pass/fail criteria
- clean records that support smarter decisions over time

## Constraints

- tight budget
- limited energy/time
- handmade production capacity
- manual platform steps still required on Facebook/Instagram
- no heavy automation until data model and process are stable

## Why AI Is Used

AI is used to:

- reduce decision fatigue
- score options before effort is spent
- draft listing/content variations quickly
- keep workflows consistent week to week
- maintain decision trails and reminders

AI is **not** used to invent fake demand or bypass real customer proof.

## Channel Summary

1. **Facebook Marketplace** = primary sales channel (cash-flow engine)
2. **Facebook Page + Instagram** = trust + content support channels
3. **TikTok/Shorts** = optional, only if content is reused cheaply
4. **Etsy** = secondary for small, shippable winners
5. **Google Ads** = later-phase testing for proven products only

See [05_CHANNEL_STRATEGY.md](05_CHANNEL_STRATEGY.md) for details.

## Operating Principles

1. Cash flow first.
2. Low-cost, high-leverage actions first.
3. Prove demand before scaling effort.
4. Prefer repeatable workflows over one-off heroics.
5. Keep human approval for major decisions and manual platform actions.
6. Automate only where safe, practical, and measurable.
7. Document decisions and assumptions clearly.
