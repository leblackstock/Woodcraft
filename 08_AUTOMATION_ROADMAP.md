# 08 Automation Roadmap

Purpose: phase automation realistically, without breaking core operations.

## Phase Now (Stabilize Foundations)

Focus: consistency before tooling.

- Standardize templates in [80_templates/](80_templates/)
- Enforce record structure from [09_DATA_MODEL.md](09_DATA_MODEL.md)
- Use AI for drafting/scoring/checklists
- Keep publishing/posting/manual buyer communication human-driven

## Phase Next (Semi-Automated Workflows)

Focus: save time on repeatable prep tasks.

- Semi-automated checklist generation for listing readiness
- Reusable content transformation workflows (listing → caption variants)
- Weekly review roll-up support from structured records
- Lightweight reminders for stale listings and backlog priorities

Dependencies:

- Reliable use of templates
- Consistent field completion in core records
- Clear weekly cadence in [11_WEEKLY_OPERATIONS.md](11_WEEKLY_OPERATIONS.md)

## Phase Later (Selective Full Automation)

Focus: automate low-risk, high-repeat tasks only.

- Automated data summaries/trend snapshots
- Assisted performance scorecards
- Optional scheduling automation where platform policy and reliability permit

Must remain manual unless explicitly approved:

- final pricing decisions
- final publish actions
- direct customer conversation
- ad budget escalation decisions

## What to Automate First

1. Repetitive drafting and formatting
2. Consistency checks against business rules
3. Weekly reporting summaries

## What to Keep Manual First

1. Product quality control
2. Buyer communication and negotiation
3. Final go/no-go decisions for listings and ads
