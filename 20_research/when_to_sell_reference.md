## Notes

These times reflect when to find products, source materials, and begin building to make marketing photos and start selling. Sounds like a lot when written out, but you got this!

Anything under $25 I do not sell on Facebook Marketplace; it involves too much wasted time on communication and flaky meetups. Smaller, pine creations are mostly sold at craft events.

To find craft shows / fairs near you:

* Search Google or Facebook for event listings.
* Attend a show and ask a few sellers about other events they attend, which are good, and which aren’t so good.
* Don’t forget to get the name/number of someone you can contact to book a vendor space.

Larger works I post on Marketplace and Etsy. I have a video about which online selling platforms cost the least in fees; a link is below.
