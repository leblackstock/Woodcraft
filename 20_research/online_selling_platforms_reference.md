## Notes

* Mercari charges $2 to transfer money from your Mercari account to your bank.
* GoImagine donates its profits to charity.
* Bonanza has free listing up to 50 items, then charges $0.03 per month for each additional item.
* Amazon Handmade will waive its monthly fee after acceptance to their handmade account.
* The platforms listed at the bottom are for building your own websites.
* If you are curious about a platform not listed on this paper, you can simply Google a phrase like `monthly fee for listing` to get a general idea of the cost before signing up.
