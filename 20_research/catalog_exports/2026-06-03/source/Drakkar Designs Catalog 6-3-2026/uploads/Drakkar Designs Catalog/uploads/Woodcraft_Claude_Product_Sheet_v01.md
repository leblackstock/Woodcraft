Use this product sheet as the clean catalog data source. Do not use older reconciliation notes, conflicting CSV values, or outdated website prices unless explicitly told to do so.

## Catalog Design Rules for Claude

- Brand: **Eggdrasil Farms**
- Product line: handmade cedar planters, raised beds, plant stands, and outdoor garden pieces.
- Style direction: polished rustic, handmade, warm, modern catalog — not a plain Word table.
- Colors: dark forest green, warm cream, cedar brown, soft moss, and muted brass accents.
- Keep the design image-forward with clean product cards.
- Use product codes as small secondary labels, not as the main names.
- Do not invent products, dimensions, prices, discounts, or materials.
- Keep customer-facing copy simple, warm, and practical.
- Leave room for product photos/renders if images are not provided.
- If making a partner-facing version, prices can be turned into blank price boxes. If making an internal/retail version, use the prices below.

## Standard Material Note

All items are locally handmade from cedar. Cedar is naturally suited for outdoor garden pieces and has a warm rustic look that fits porch, patio, and homestead-style displays.
