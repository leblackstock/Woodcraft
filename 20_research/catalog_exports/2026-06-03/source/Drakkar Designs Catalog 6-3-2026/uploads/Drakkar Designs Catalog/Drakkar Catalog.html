§ 01 — Our Wood, Our Hand

Cedar that
earns its place
on a porch.

Drakkar Designs is a small homestead workshop building garden and porch goods from western red cedar — rot-resistant, aromatic, and milled within a hundred miles of the shop.

Every piece is cut, sanded, jointed and finished by hand. Joinery is built to outlast a single season. Finish is food-safe tung oil or unfinished, by request.

Studio · raw cedar stock PHOTO_01
Materials

Western red cedar, stainless fasteners, food-safe finishes.

Made
