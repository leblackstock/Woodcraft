§ 01 — The Shop Behind the Mark

One man,
one bench,
one project at a time.

A one-man cedar shop out of Lovejoy, Georgia. Every piece cut, fit, and finished by the same set of hands. Self-taught, been at the bench since he was a kid.

Everything's western red cedar. Rot resistant, smells good, and ages to a soft silver if you leave it outside. Nothing ships out until it’s just right.
