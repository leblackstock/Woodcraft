§ 07 — Rebuild in Canva

Canva
recreate guide.

A 12-step path from this PDF to an editable Canva Brand Kit + template set.
A · Brand Kit setup (one-time)
1
Create a Brand Kit named Drakkar Designs Canva → Brand → New brand kit. Use this kit for every Drakkar doc.
2
Load the six brand colors #13181B #414444 #A4A9A5 #DEDCD6 #CF4E17 #E04B1A
3
Set the three brand fonts Heading: Cormorant Garamond (Light 300). Body: Manrope (Reg 400 / Semi 600). Label: JetBrains Mono (Reg 400). All three are in Canva's Google Fonts.
4
Upload brand assets Monogram D (SVG), Drakkar wordmark (SVG), corner registration mark, hatched image-placeholder PNG. Tag each under brand / drakkar.
5
Create a US Letter document, 8.5 × 11 in, portrait Page setup → margins 0.625 in. Save as a Template after page 1.
B · Build the page templates
6
Master Page — runners & furniture Top runner: DRAKKAR · DESIGNS in Cormorant (10pt, tracked +420). Bottom runner: page # + section. Both in Charcoal #414444. Copy onto every page.
7
Cover page Background fill #13181B. Wordmark Cormorant Light 120pt, the period in italic #CF4E17. Add the four corner registration marks (SVG).
8
Product card component (reusable) Group: image frame (square, 1:1), SKU label (mono orange), serif title, spec list (mono key / sans value), price box. Save as an Element → reuse for all 17 SKUs.
9
Price box element Rectangle 2px orange stroke, no fill. Add small stone-fill squares at top-left and bottom-right for the cut-corner tabs. Inside: mono label, underline rule, mono note.
10
Order & pricing tables Use the Table element. Header row 2px black border top/bottom, mono small caps. Body rows 1px #1318181B@18% bottom border. Alternate row 2.5% black for internal pricing.
11
Two file outputs Duplicate the master into Drakkar — Partner Catalog (pages 1–9) and Drakkar — Internal Sheet (pages 10–11). Lock layers so partner copy can't be edited by a buyer.
12
Export Share → Download → PDF Print, Crop marks + bleed off, CMYK if going to print, Flatten PDF on. Save partner + internal as separate PDFs.
Watch-outs Cormorant Garamond Light 300 is the right weight — don't let Canva auto-substitute Medium. Burnt orange is #CF4E17, never the vivid #E04B1A for body text. Keep image frames as the hatched placeholder until real photos land.

A · What this is

A printable wholesale catalog & order packet for Drakkar Designs — a handmade cedar / homestead workshop. Designed as a US Letter portrait PDF, partner-facing pages 1–9 and internal pages 10–11, with a separate system & handoff doc.

B · Visual direction

Boutique line-sheet meets rural maker. Stone-grey page, charcoal & near-black for headers and the back-office section, burnt orange as the only accent. Generous whitespace, serif headings, mono micro-labels, hand-drawn rules.

C · Locked palette
#13181B#414444#A4A9A5 #DEDCD6#CF4E17#E04B1A
Do not substitute or add hues. All on-brand neutrals derive from these via opacity.
D · Type pairing

Cormorant Garamond Light 300 (headings + pull quotes, italic for emphasis), Manrope 400/500/600 (body + UI), JetBrains Mono 400/500 (SKUs, micro-labels, totals).

E · Packet contents (14 pages)
01 Cover · 02 Brand · 03 Catalog opener · 04–07 Products A → Q (17 SKUs, 9 categories) · 08 Variations · 09 Partner order sheet · 10 Internal divider · 11 Retail price sheet · 12 Design system · 13 Canva build guide · 14 This brief.
F · Two output files
  • Drakkar — Partner Catalog: pages 01–09. Distribute freely.
  • Drakkar — Internal Sheet: pages 10–11 + 12–14. Never send to partners.
G · Reusable components
  • Product card — image / SKU / title / specs / price box (mirror left/right for rhythm).
  • Price box — 2px orange frame, cut-corner tabs, signature blank.
  • Order & price tables — mono small-cap heads, hairline body rules.
  • Runners — Cormorant mark top-left, mono section right; mono page # bottom.
