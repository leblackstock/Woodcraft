This is the visual direction for Facebook Marketplace image prompt packs. It should keep generated graphics and branded lifestyle images consistent with the Drakkar catalog while still letting product photos feel warm, natural, and sales-friendly.

Any brand-specific graphics, generated visuals, HTML, templates, ads, or prompt packs should reference `00_brand/` for current logos, approved product photos, palette notes, and brand asset provenance.

## Preferred Feel

- Match the original catalog colors, especially black/charcoal, warm stone, muted silver, and cedar/burnt orange.
- Do not force every image to be very dark. Dark brand cards are useful, but lifestyle and product images can use daylight, porch warmth, greenery, stone/cream backgrounds, and visible cedar color.
- Keep the product wood warm and cedar-forward, with realistic grain and a handcrafted feel.
- Use dark/charcoal backgrounds mainly for price cards, brand/order cards, or dramatic hero composites.
- Use light stone/cream backgrounds when a cleaner or more readable Marketplace image would work better.
- Avoid bright unrelated colors, neon palettes, glossy luxury styling, generic stock-photo blues, and washed-out beige that loses the Drakkar identity.
